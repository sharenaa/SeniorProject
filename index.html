Why Awareness Matters

Root Causes of the Teen Mental Health Crisis

📱 Attention & Addiction by Design

Notifications, likes, streaks, and endless scrolling can make it difficult to disconnect and recharge.

😔 Social Comparison

Comparing ourselves to curated online lives can contribute to anxiety, loneliness, and lower self-esteem.

💬 Cyberbullying

Online harassment can impact mental health long after an interaction ends.

Who Is Affected?

Teenagers

Mental health, academics, relationships, and self-esteem.

Families

Supporting loved ones and navigating resources.

Schools

Growing need for counselors and wellness programs.
